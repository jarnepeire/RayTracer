A quick overview of implementations in the raytracer project:
- Week 1-5 assignments
- Multithreaded rendering
- SceneManager system (makes it possible to swap between scenes in realtime)

The key bind inputs: (you can find these printed in the console at the start too)

| Display KeyBind Information          |  I  |

+--------------------------------------+-----+	
| Enable / Disable Left Point Light    |  K  |	
+--------------------------------------+-----+
| Enable / Disable Right Point Light   |  L  |	
+--------------------------------------+-----+	
| Enable / Disable Hard Shadows        |  Z  |	
+--------------------------------------+-----+
